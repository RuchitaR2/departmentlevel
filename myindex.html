About Department

Electronics and Computer Science

The Department was established in 1987 with B.E. in Electronics and Computer Science. From the academic year 2019-2020,the course was renamed to Electronics And Computer Science to cope up with 4th Industrial revolution.

After a comprehensive study of the industry requirements as well as technological trends, the syllabus for the course Electronics and Computer Science has been framed to include courses that build a conceptual understanding of basics of Electronics and Computer Science.Additional courses like Machine Learning,VLSI and Data Science have been incorporated.
